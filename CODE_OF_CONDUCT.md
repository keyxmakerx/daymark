# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version
2.1, as its code of conduct.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy and
kindness, being respectful of differing opinions, giving and gracefully accepting constructive
feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes
harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, publishing others' private information,
and other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
project maintainers through GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

The full text and enforcement guidelines are available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
